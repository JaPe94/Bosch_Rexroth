How to Use AR Content

Follow these steps to enjoy Augmented Reality content on your smartphone:

For Android

  • Ensure your device supports ARCore (most modern Android devices do).
  • Open the provided link in Chrome or another AR-compatible browser.
  • Grant permissions to access your camera when prompted.
  • Point your camera at a flat surface to anchor the AR content.

For iPhone

  • Ensure your device supports ARKit (iPhone 6S or later).
  • Open the link in Safari for the best experience.
  • Allow access to your camera when asked.
  • Point your camera at a flat surface to place the AR object.
